中国

China，officially the People's Republic of China，is a country in East Asia. It is the world's most populous country, with a population of more than 1.4 billion. 中国，全称中华人民共和国，是东亚国家。它是世界上人口最多的国家，人口超过14亿。

Shanghai Jiao Tong University, which is one of the oldest Chinese University, was build in 1896. 上海交通大学是中国最古老的大学之一，始建于1896年。

威神V

WayV (Chinese: 威神V, pronounced as "WeiShenV") is a seven-member boy group formed by SM Entertainment, and the fourth overall sub-unit of NCT. They debuted in China on January 17, 2019 with their first digital EP, The Vision. WayV（中文：威神V）是由SM娱乐组建的七人男团，NCT总分队第四。他们于 2019 年 1 月 17 日在中国首次亮相，发行了他们的首张数字 EP，The Vision。
